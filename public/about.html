about the calculator

335 swipes. three meals a day. how hard could it be to use them all?

very, as it turned out. at the end of the 2024 fall semester, some friends had upwards of a hundred and fifty swipes. what could've helped? none other than a calculator!

what started as a messy spreadsheet evolved into this site. as it helped me get down to one meal swipe (a whole other story), i hope it can help you as well :)

any questions/comments? bluecicada@duck.com

technical details, for those interested

  • break calculation: the calculation for breaks is exclusive of the end day. if you have a break from 2/20 to 2/25, for instance, the break is calculated as 5 days--not 6.

  • calendar input: typing multiple dates seems a bit tedious. might add a calendar api. keep an eye out!

known bugs

  • break options dropdown malfunctioning when Safari is zoomed in to specific values
