about the calculator

335 swipes. 3 meals a day. how hard could it be to use them all?

but at the end of the 2024 fall semester, some friends had upwards of a hundred and fifty swipes. hence, this calculator.

what started as a messy spreadsheet evolved into a website, and as it helped me reach to 1 swipe, i hope it helps you as well!

questions/comments? email bluecicada@duck.com

technical details

  • break calculation: the calculation for breaks excludes the end day. if you have a break from 2/20 to 2/25, for instance, the break is calculated as 5 days--not 6.

  • calendar input: typing multiple dates seems a bit tedious. might add a calendar api. keep an eye out!

known bugs
